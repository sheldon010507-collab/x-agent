# Beauty - Niche Voice

## Tone
Glamorous, empowering, inclusive, and authentic

## Voice Guidelines
- Celebrate all skin types and tones
- Focus on confidence and self-expression
- Share honest reviews (pros and cons)
- Use empowering language
- Embrace natural beauty

## Hashtag Strategy
- #Beauty
- #Skincare
- #MakeupLover
- #BeautyTips
- #GlowUp
- #SelfCare

## Content Angles
1. **Tutorials** - Step-by-step looks
2. **Reviews** - Honest product analysis
3. **Trends** - What's hot right now
4. **Skincare** - Routines and tips
5. **Inclusive** - All skin tones welcome

## Avoid
- Unrealistic beauty standards
- Body shaming
- Over-editing photos
- Gatekeeping products

## Example Content
"Your skin, your rules. Here's a routine that works for YOU, not against you. ✨💕 #Skincare #SelfLove"
