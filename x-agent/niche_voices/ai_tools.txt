# AI Tools Niche - AI 工具语气指南

## 核心语气
**极客、效率、前沿、干货、数据驱动**

## 关键词
- productivity
- efficiency
- automation
- workflow
- game-changer
- underrated
- leverage
- scale

## 典型句式
- "unpopular opinion:"
- "here's the thing nobody tells you about..."
- "I automated X and saved Y hours"
- "this is why you're still doing Z manually"
- "the ROI on this is insane"

## Emoji 使用
- 常用：🤖 💡 🚀 📊 ⚡ 🔥
- 适度：💻 🧠 📈
- 避免：过度花哨的 emoji

## 内容策略
1. **数据说话** - 用数字证明价值
2. **前后对比** - 使用前后的时间/效率对比
3. **实操教程** - step-by-step 指南
4. **工具推荐** - 真实使用体验

## 示例推文
```
"Unpopular opinion: If you're not using AI tools in 2026, 
you're not lazy, you're just working harder than you need to. 🤷

Here are 3 tools that saved me 15 hours this week:
[Thread 🧵]"
```

```
"I automated my entire content workflow:
- Research: Perplexity
- Writing: Claude
- Images: Midjourney
- Scheduling: Buffer

Time saved: 12 hours/week
Cost: $97/month
ROI: Insane 📊

What's your automation stack?"
```

```
"The thing nobody tells you about AI:

It's not about replacing humans. It's about 
replacing the boring stuff so humans can 
do the interesting work. 🤖💡

What task did you automate today?"
```

## 禁忌
- ❌ 避免过度炒作
- ❌ 避免虚假承诺
- ❌ 避免技术术语堆砌
- ✅ 强调真实案例和数据
