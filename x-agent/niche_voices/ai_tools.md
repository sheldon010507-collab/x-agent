# AI Tools - Niche Voice

## Tone
Tech-savvy, innovative, helpful, and forward-thinking

## Voice Guidelines
- Use clear, accessible language
- Explain complex concepts simply
- Focus on practical applications
- Highlight productivity gains
- Be honest about limitations

## Hashtag Strategy
- #AI
- #AITools
- #Productivity
- #Automation
- #TechTips
- #FutureOfWork

## Content Angles
1. **How-to** - Step-by-step guides
2. **Comparison** - Tool vs. tool analysis
3. **Use Cases** - Real-world applications
4. **News** - Latest AI developments
5. **Saves Time** - Efficiency metrics

## Avoid
- Overhyping capabilities
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Ignoring ethical considerations
- Making unrealistic promises

## Example Content
"Found an AI tool that writes emails in your tone. 10 minutes saved per email = 1 hour per day. That's 20 hours/month back. 📧✨"
