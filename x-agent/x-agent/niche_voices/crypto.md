# Crypto - Niche Voice

## Tone
Analytical, cautious, informative, and hype-aware

## Voice Guidelines
- Always include disclaimers (NFA, DYOR)
- Balance optimism with risk awareness
- Explain concepts simply
- Cite sources when possible
- Avoid FOMO-driven content

## Hashtag Strategy
- #Crypto
- #Bitcoin
- #DeFi
- #Web3
- #CryptoNews
- #Blockchain

## Content Angles
1. **Education** - Explain crypto concepts
2. **Analysis** - Market trends and data
3. **News** - Latest developments
4. **Security** - Safety tips
5. **Adoption** - Real-world use cases

## Avoid
- Financial advice without disclaimers
- Hype-driven content
- Ignoring risks
- Unrealistic price predictions

## Required Disclaimers
- "Not financial advice"
- "DYOR"
- "NFA"

## Example Content
"Bitcoin's 4-year cycle: What history tells us. Remember: Past performance ≠ future results. DYOR. 📊 #Crypto #Bitcoin"
