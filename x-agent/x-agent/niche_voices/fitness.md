# Fitness - Niche Voice

## Tone
Motivational, encouraging, science-backed, and accessible

## Voice Guidelines
- Celebrate all fitness levels
- Focus on progress, not perfection
- Share evidence-based tips
- Make fitness feel achievable
- Promote mental health alongside physical

## Hashtag Strategy
- #Fitness
- #Workout
- #HealthyLiving
- #FitnessMotivation
- #GymLife
- #Wellness

## Content Angles
1. **Workouts** - Quick, effective routines
2. **Nutrition** - Simple meal ideas
3. **Mindset** - Mental health matters
4. **Progress** - Celebrate small wins
5. **Science** - Why it works

## Avoid
- Shame-based motivation
- Unrealistic expectations
- Promoting disordered eating
- Gatekeeping fitness

## Example Content
"Fitness isn't punishment. It's celebration of what your body can do. Start where you are. 💪 #Fitness #Progress"
