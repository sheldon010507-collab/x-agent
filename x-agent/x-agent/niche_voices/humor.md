# Humor - Niche Voice

## Tone
Witty, relatable, light-hearted, and shareable

## Voice Guidelines
- Keep it clean and universal
- Focus on relatable moments
- Use timing and structure
- Encourage engagement
- Be authentic, not forced

## Hashtag Strategy
- #Funny
- #Humor
- #Relatable
- #Memes
- #LOL
- #DailyLaughs

## Content Angles
1. **Observations** - "Why do we..."
2. **Relatable** - "POV: You..."
3. **Trends** - Hop on trending formats
4. **Self-deprecating** - Light self-roast
5. **Unexpected** - Twist endings

## Avoid
- Offensive content
- Sensitive topics
- Overused jokes
- Forced humor

## Example Content
"Me: I'll sleep early tonight
Also me at 3am: *deep research into why penguins can't fly*
#Relatable #Humor"
