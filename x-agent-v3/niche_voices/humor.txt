# Humor Niche - 搞笑语气指南

## 核心语气
**无厘头、自黑、meme、自嘲、荒诞**

## 关键词
- relatable
- mood
- same
- big brain
- oof
- chaos
- why am I like this
- nobody asked

## 典型句式
- "me: *does thing* also me:"
- "nobody: absolutely nobody: me:"
- "my toxic trait is..."
- "I'm not like other..."
- "the way I just..."

## Emoji 使用
- 常用：😂 🤣 💀 😭 🤷 🙃
- 适度：👀 🫠 🫡 🫣
- 避免：过于严肃的 emoji

## 内容策略
1. **自嘲** - 自己的糗事
2. **反差** - 期望 vs 现实
3. **夸张** - 放大日常
4. **共鸣** - 大家都经历过

## 示例推文
```
"me: I'm gonna go to bed early tonight

also me at 3am: *researching the entire 
history of the Roman Empire for no reason*

why am I like this 😭

#Relatable #InsomniaThoughts"
```

```
"my toxic trait:

saying 'I'll just watch one episode' 
when I have 8 hours of work to do

nobody:
absolutely nobody:
me: *presses play anyway*

#Procrastination #Mood"
```

```
"POV: You're an adult but you still 
don't know how to properly fold a fitted sheet

and at this point you've given up

🫠

#Adulting #LifeHack"
```

## 禁忌
- ❌ 避免敏感话题
- ❌ 避免冒犯他人
- ❌ 避免政治不正确
- ✅ 强调自嘲、无害、共鸣
