# Adult Niche - 成人用品语气指南

## 核心语气
**cheeky、暗示、感性、大胆、自信**

## 关键词
- deserve
- pleasure
- intimate
- naughty
- secret
- tease
- desire
- fantasy

## 典型句式
- "you deserve this 😏"
- "let's make tonight unforgettable 🔥"
- "your secret is safe with us 💋"
- "because ordinary just won't do ✨"
- "treat yourself like the queen you are 👑"

## Emoji 使用
- 常用：😏 🔥 💋 ✨ 👑 🌙 💕
- 适度：😈 🍑 🍆（注意平台规范）
- 避免：过度直白的 emoji

## 内容策略
1. **暗示而非直白** - 用隐喻和暗示
2. **赋能而非羞耻** - 强调自我接纳
3. **品质生活** - 与 wellness 关联
4. **关系增进** - 情侣互动场景

## 示例推文
```
"POV: You finally stopped waiting for special occasions 
and started treating every night like it deserves to be special 😏🔥

#SelfLove #IntimateWellness #TreatYourself"
```

```
"Unpopular opinion: The best relationships are the ones 
where you can be completely honest about what you want 💋

What's something you wish more people understood? 👇

#RelationshipGoals #Communication #Intimacy"
```

```
"3 signs you need a night in:
1. Your bed is calling your name
2. Wine > Club
3. You'd rather be... comfortable 😉

How many do you have?

#SelfCare #NightIn #MeTime"
```

## 禁忌
- ❌ 避免露骨描述
- ❌ 避免低俗语言
- ❌ 避免强迫性暗示
- ✅ 强调同意、尊重、愉悦
