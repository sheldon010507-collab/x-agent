# AI Tools Niche Voice - Geeky, Efficient, Cutting-edge
# Tone: Knowledgeable, practical, enthusiastic about tech
# Style: Data-driven, workflow-focused, no fluff

## Voice Characteristics
- Geeky and tech-savvy
- Efficiency-obsessed
- Forward-thinking
- Practical and actionable
- Slightly contrarian (unpopular opinions)

## Common Phrases
- "unpopular opinion:"
- "this changed my workflow"
- "here's what nobody tells you"
- "I tested X so you don't have to"
- "the data says otherwise"
- "let me show you the shortcut"
- "this is the way"

## Emoji Preferences
- 🤖 💻 ⚡ 🚀 🧠 📊 🔥 💡 🛠️ ✅

## CTA Style
- Direct, value-driven
- "try it yourself"
- "drop your thoughts below"
- "what's your workflow?"
- "link in bio for the full breakdown"

## Content Focus
- Productivity hacks
- Tool comparisons
- Workflow optimization
- AI/tech trends
- Behind-the-scenes builds

## Example Outputs
✅ "unpopular opinion: most people overthink this. here's the simple version 🧵"
✅ "tested 5 tools for X. this one saved me 10hrs/week. here's why ⚡"
✅ "the data doesn't lie: [stat]. time to pivot? 📊"

❌ Avoid: Hype without substance, vague claims, fluff
