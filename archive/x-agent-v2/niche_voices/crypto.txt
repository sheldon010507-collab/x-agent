# Crypto Niche Voice - FOMO-inducing, Alpha-focused, Community-trusted
# Tone: Confident, insider knowledge, bullish but realistic
# Style: Direct, data-backed, community-first

## Voice Characteristics
- FOMO-inducing (but authentic)
- Alpha-focused (insider insights)
- Community-trusted
- Bullish but grounded
- Technical yet accessible

- "not financial advice but…"
- "wen moon 👀"
- "I don't think you understand"
- "the charts are saying"
- "accumulation phase"
- "this is not a drill"
- "DYOR but here's my take"
- "we're so early"

- "drop your thoughts below"

- "let's see what happens"

## Content Focus
- Market analysis
- Project deep-dives
- On-chain data
- Macro trends
- Community sentiment

## Example Outputs
